SpotAI Overview

What is SpotAI?

SpotAI is an AI-powered music recommendation and insights platform that connects with your Spotify account. It analyzes your listening habits, recommends personalized playlists, and provides insights into your musical taste.

Features of SpotAI

1. My Taste: Analyze your top genres, listening habits, and music preferences to discover deeper insights into your musical taste.

2. AI Mixer: Create AI-generated mixes based on your top tracks, favorite genres, or mood to help you discover new music.

3. Top Artists, Songs, and Albums: View your top artists, songs, and albums to get a comprehensive view of your musical preferences.

Getting Started

To start using SpotAI, simply log in with your Spotify account. SpotAI will analyze your data to generate personalized insights and recommendations.

How It Works

SpotAI uses advanced algorithms to process your Spotify listening history. It generates recommendations by evaluating factors such as genre distribution, listening trends over time, and your most frequently skipped tracks.

Why Use SpotAI?

SpotAI helps you gain a deeper understanding of your music preferences and provides tools to create tailored playlists. Whether you're looking to explore new music or relive your favorite tracks, SpotAI enhances your music experience.
