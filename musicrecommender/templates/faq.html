Frequently Asked Questions

What is SpotAI? +
SpotAI is an AI-powered application that enhances your Spotify music experience by analyzing your music preferences, generating personalized playlists, and providing insights into your listening habits.
How do I log in to SpotAI? +
To log in, click the "Login to Spotify" button on the home page and connect your Spotify account. If you do not have an account, you can register through the "Create new account" option.
What kind of data does SpotAI analyze? +
SpotAI analyzes your Spotify listening data, including your top artists, songs, albums, favorite genres, listening habits by decade, and more. It uses this data to offer insights and recommendations tailored to your preferences.
How does the AI Mixer work? +
The AI Mixer feature will generate personalized playlists by analyzing your music preferences and combining them with other factors like mood and energy levels. This feature is coming soon!
How is my data used? +
SpotAI only uses your Spotify data to personalize your music experience. Your data is not shared with third parties, and you can disconnect your Spotify account at any time.
Can I switch between light and dark mode?+
Yes, SpotAI allows you to toggle between light and dark mode from the settings page. This theme setting will be applied across all pages.
Why can’t I see the ‘Mix Now’ or ‘My Mixes’ features yet?+
These features are currently under development and will be available soon. Please check back for updates!
How do I reset my password?+
If you’ve forgotten your password, click on the "Forgotten account?" link on the login page. You will receive an email with instructions to reset your password.
How do I change my Spotify data preferences?+
To change the data SpotAI accesses, you can update your Spotify account’s permissions under the Spotify app settings. Once updated, SpotAI will only use the data you've allowed access to.
Who developed SpotAI?+
SpotAI was developed by Husain Wafaie, a software engineer who specializes in full-stack development and artificial intelligence applications.
